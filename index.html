⏱️ Chemical Kinetics ~3–4 Qs confirmed in May 19 shift
  • Rate law: r = k[A]ˣ[B]ʸ — order from experiment, NOT stoichiometry
  • Half-life (1st order): t½ = 0.693/k — constant regardless of concentration
  • Arrhenius equation: k = Ae^(−Ea/RT) — log k vs 1/T gives −Ea/2.303R slope
  • If t½ is constant → first order. If t½ doubles → zero order. If t½ halves → second order.

🌊 Solutions ~3–4 Qs confirmed high weightage May 19
  • Raoult's law: p = x₁p°₁ — relative lowering VP = x₂ (mole fraction of solute)
  • Osmotic pressure: π = iCRT (i = van't Hoff; NaCl i=2, CaCl₂ i=3)
  • ΔTb = iKbm and ΔTf = iKfm — use MOLALITY (mol/kg of solvent), not molarity!
  • Positive deviation (A-B < A-A, B-B) → vapour pressure > ideal → acetone+CS₂
